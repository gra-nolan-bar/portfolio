My Hotel Management Journey

I started out in hotel management in 2013 at a beach-front resort in Laguna Beach. I worked as a front desk agent for two years, and a bell captain for another two years. I moved to Portland, OR in 2017 and worked at a few hotels in the downtown Portland area, including the opening of a new hotel in late 2018. My favorite part about working in hotels has always been on the software side of the job. I love poking around in the configuration settings of our PMS and other systems. It really sparked my interest in the tech industry and I hope to someday soon make the career change. I am looking forward to this first major step in the right direction by taking the UW Front-End Web Developer Certification in 2024!
